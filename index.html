A mathematician by training, data scientist by trade, writer by nature, reader for leisure, runner, expresser of opinions, and someone who just wants to solve problems using data and also write fiction.

Recently, I've been spending my time with software start-ups, co-founding and advising, after a long stint in academia, where the work is statistically more significant but the tangible results are somewhat less significant. After realizing that typical data science and Big Data need to brush up on their applications of statistical methods, I decided to do my best to help out.

I'm what you might call a "full stack" data scientist, but I don't think anyone should be employed as such. You don't want one guy doing the UI and the machine learning. Sure, we can do both, but one of them isn't going to look good.

Motto of the moment: Lots of people are good at telling you things that you can do with data science, but I want to tell you what you should do.

Besides data and numbers and software (oh my!), I like writing, taking pictures (see some of my favorites here), reading (mainly fiction), and performing arts (mainly spectating). I can make a mean cocktail and love getting involved in anything creative, artful, or stylish.

I'm interested in almost everything. If you have a cool project or event, or you're interested in hearing about mine, don't hesitate to contact me via any of the channels at the bottom of the page.
